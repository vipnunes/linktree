Google Chrome

Google Chrome (Windows)

How to disable all cookies:


1) Click in Chrome menu icon;

2) go to Settings > Site Settings > Cookies and site data;

3) Deselect Allow sites to save and read cookie data (recommended).


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Click in the Chrome menu icon;

2) go to Settings > Site Settings > Cookies and site data;

3) Select the option: "Block third-party cookies".

Google Chrome (Mac OS)

How to disable all cookies:


1) Click in Chrome menu icon;

2) go to Settings;

3) under the “Privacy and security”, select "Cookies and other site data";

4) Check the option "Block All Cookies".


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Click in Chrome menu icon;

2) go to Settings;

3) under the “Privacy and security”, select "Cookies and other site data";

4) Check Block third-party cookies.

Google Chrome (Android)

How to disable all cookies:

1) Tap Menu > Settings > Site settings.

2) Tap Cookies.

3) Tap the slider to turn cookies off.

How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Tap Menu > Settings > Site settings.

2) Tap Cookies.

3) Check Block third-party cookies.

How to disable all cookies:

4) Check the option "Block All Cookies".


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Click in Chrome menu icon;

2) go to Settings;

Firefox

Firefox (Windows)

How to disable all cookies:

1) Click on the More actions button in the top right corner and select Options.

2) Click Privacy & Security on the left sidebar.

3) Under Enhanced Tracking Protection, select Custom.

4) In the Cookies drop-down list, select All cookies (will cause websites to break).

How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Click on the More actions button in the top right corner and select Options.

2) Click Privacy & Security on the left sidebar.

3) Under Enhanced Tracking Protection, select Custom.

4) In the Cookies drop-down list, select All third-party cookies (may cause websites to break).

Firefox (Mac OS)

How to disable all cookies:


1) Click on the hamburger button in the top right corner and select Preferences.

2) Click the Privacy & Security panel.

3) Under Enhanced Tracking Protection, select the Custom radio button 

4) Select the Cookies checkbox.

5) The default setting is Cross site and social media trackers. Change the selection in the drop-down list to All cookies (will cause websites to break).


How to disable third-party cookies:

1) Click on the hamburger button in the top right corner and select Preferences.

2) Click the Privacy & Security panel.

3) Under Enhanced Tracking Protection, select the Custom radio button

4) Select the Cookies checkbox.

5) The default setting is Cross site and social media trackers. Change the selection in the drop-down list to All third-party cookies (may cause websites to break).


Firefox (Android)

How to disable all cookies:


1) Tap Menu > Settings > Privacy.

2) Tap Cookies.

3) Tap Disabled.


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Tap Menu > Settings > Privacy.

2) Tap Cookies.

3) Tap Enabled, excluding 3rd party.

Firefox (iOS)

How to disable all cookies:


1) Tap the Menu > Settings > Data Management.

2) Tap the Cookies slider to disable.


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Firefox on iOS now blocks all third-party cookies by default.

Firefox (Linux)

How to disable all cookies:


1) Click on the More actions button in the top right corner and select Preferences.

2) Click Privacy & Security.

3) Under Content Blocking, choose the Custom radio button.

4) Select the checkbox next to Cookies

5) Click the drop-down menu next to Cookies and select All cookies (will cause websites to break).


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Click on the More actions button in the top right corner and select Preferences.

2) Click Privacy & Security.

3) Under Content Blocking, choose the Custom radio button.

4) Select the checkbox next to Cookies

5) Click the drop-down menu next to Cookies and select All third-party cookies (may cause websites to break).

Safari

Safari (Mac OS)

How to disable all cookies:
1) Choose Safari > Preferences

2) Click Privacy

3) Select Block all cookies.


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Safari now blocks all third-party cookies by default.

Safari (iOS)

How to disable all cookies:

1) Open the phone’s Settings app.

2) Select Safari.

3) Scross down to Privacy & Security.

4) Select Block Cookies.

5) Select Block All from the confirmation prompt.


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Safari now blocks all third-party cookies by default.

Brave

Brave (Mac OS)

How to disable all cookies:

1) Click the hamburger button in the top right corner and select Settings

2) Click Shields in the left sidebar

3) Set the Cookie blocking dropdown menu to All.


How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Brave now blocks all third-party cookies by default.

Internet Explorer

Internet Explorer (Windows)

How to disable all cookies:

1) Select the gear in the upper-right corner of the screen, then select Internet Options. If you have the Menu Bar enabled, you can select Tools > Internet Options.

2) Click the Privacy tab.

3) Select the Advanced button.

4) Under First-party Cookies and Third-party Cookies, choose Block to automatically block cookies or Prompt to prompt with each cookie request.

5) Click OK and you have successfully set your cookie settings

How to disable third-party cookies:


1) Select the gear in the upper-right corner of the screen, then select Internet Options. If you have the Menu Bar enabled, you can select Tools > Internet Options.

2) Click the Privacy tab.

3) Select the Advanced button.

4) Under Third-party Cookies, choose Block to automatically block cookies or Prompt to prompt with each cookie request.

5) Click OK and you have successfully set your cookie settings in IE11.
